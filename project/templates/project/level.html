Levels

A1

Students at this level can understand and use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases aimed at satisfying concrete needs such as introducing themselves and others. They can interact in a simple way provided the other person talks slowly and clearly and is prepared to help.

The time duration for this course is 4 weeks.

A2

Students at this level can understand sentences and frequently used expressions related to areas of most immediate relevence such as basic personal and family information, shopping and work. They can exchange information on familiar and routine matters and describe in simple terms aspects of their background and immediate environment

The time duration for this course is 5 weeks.

B1

Students can understand the main points of clear standard input on familiar matters such as work, school, leisure etc. They can deal with more situations whilst travelling in Spanish speaking areas. They can describe experiences and events, dreams, hopes and ambitions and give brief explanations for opinions and plans.

The time duration for this course is 7 weeks.

B2

Students should be able to understand the main ideas of complex texts on both concrete and abstract topics, including technical discussions in the field of specialisation. They can interact with the degree of fluency that makes regular interaction possible.

The time duration for this course is 8 weeks.

C1

Students should be able to understand and recognise a wide range of implicit meanings. They can express themselves fluently and spontaneously without much obvious seraching for expressions. They can use language flexibly and effectively for social, acedemic and proffesional purposes.

The time duration for this course is 11 weeks

C2

Students can understand with ease virtually everything heard or read. They can summarise information from different spoken and written sources, reconstructing arguments and accounts in a coherent presentation. They are able to express themselves spontaneosly, very fluently anf precisely, differentiating final shades of meaning even in more complex situations.

The time duration for this course is 13 weeks.
